Rules and Regulations:
1. All residents must maintain a clean and tidy living environment. Regular cleaning of rooms and common areas is mandatory.

9. No cooking is allowed in the rooms.

11. Any harassment or discrimination of other residents based on their race, religion, gender, or sexual orientation is strictly prohibited.

12. Residents vacating before the notice period will not be getting their deposits back.

13. Any violation of hostel rules and regulations may result in residents agreeing to not return their deposits, or includes fines, suspension, or expulsion from the hostel.

14. Any complaint from the neighbours/society will result in severe action.

15. Before leaving the PG Accommodation, a guest must pay all dues and hand over the charges of rooms and other material in satisfactory condition to the owner of the premises.
